Step Dance Revolution whatever pre alpha

Instructions for installation
1. Place tstep.h and tstep.c, and the .dwi file into the podzilla directory.
2. Goto http://www.ipodlinux.org/Modifying_Podzilla
3. Scroll down to "The podzilla menu system"
4. Follow their directions, except whenever "steroids" is mentioned, replace that with "tstep".
5. Also follow the directions for Building podzilla.
